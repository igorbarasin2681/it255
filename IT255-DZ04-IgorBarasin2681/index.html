Hostel King is located in a residential area of Belgrade city in a quiet street, surrounded by churches, monuments, markets, museums and historically significant touristic sights. The Hostel is 1 km far from the Slavija Square, 2.7 km from the Republic Square, and 0.6 km from St. Sava temple. The nearest bus stop with lines to the centre is 100 meters from Hostel King. The main train station is 2.5 km away. Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport is at a distance of 22 km and shuttle service can be arranged at a surcharge. The hostel offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and free private parking. All rooms come with parquet floors and offer garden views. Each features lockers. A common living area and shared bathrooms with showers are at guests’ disposal. We are able to accommodate groups up to 25 people. Hostel King provides breakfast service. The nearest grocery store is 50 meters away, the nearest bakery is in 2 minutes walk, and a farmer market is in 5 minutes walk. Kitchen is available for customers’ use. Hostel King has a large garden , which is the most beautiful garden of all the hostels in Belgrade, with endemic species of trees in the yard that includes a barbecue area and sits 50 people where guests can enjoy food & drinks served by cafe King.
